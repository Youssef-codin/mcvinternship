Spaghetti Carbonara

Recipe Overview

This classic italian pasta dish is rich, creamy and full of flavor.

It only takes 20 minutes to prepare and is perfect for a quick meal. (serves 3 people)

Ingredients

  • 200g Spaghetti
  • 2 Eggs
  • 1/2 cup
  • Salt and black pepper

Cooking steps

  1. Boil spaghetti in salted water until...
  2. Cook until crispy
  3. Mix eggs and cheese in a bowl
  4. Combine everything with drained pasta while hot
  5. Season with salt and pepper

Tips

Make sure teh pasta is hot when you mix in the egg and cheese mixture for a creamy
